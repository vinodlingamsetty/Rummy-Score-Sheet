# Privacy Policy

**Rummy Scorekeeper** ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

---

## Data We Collect

- **User ID** – Unique identifier from Firebase Authentication
- **Display name** – Public name you choose for in-game use
- **Email** – Optional; provided only if you sign in with Apple or Google and choose to share it
- **Game data** – Scores, rooms, player roster, game history
- **Friend/balance data** – Settlements, due amounts, and payment tracking between players
- **Crash data** – Crash reports and diagnostics via Firebase Crashlytics to fix bugs
- **Analytics** – Anonymous usage events (e.g., feature usage) via Firebase Analytics
- **Device identifier** – Used for push notifications when you enable them (for Nudge reminders)

---

## How We Use It

- Track scores and sync game state across devices
- Calculate settlements and manage balances between friends
- Improve app stability and fix crashes
- Understand how the app is used to improve the experience

---

## Third Parties

We use:

- **Firebase** (Google) – Authentication, Firestore (cloud database), Analytics, Crashlytics
- **Apple** – Sign in with Apple

These services have their own privacy policies. Data is processed according to their terms and our instructions.

---

## Sign In Options

- **Sign in with Apple** – Uses your Apple ID
- **Sign in with Google** – Uses your Google account (when available)
- **Sign in anonymously** – For development/testing only; not available in production builds

---

## Permissions

- **Camera** – Used to scan QR codes when joining a game
- **Notifications** – Used to send Nudge reminders; only sent if you enable them in Settings
