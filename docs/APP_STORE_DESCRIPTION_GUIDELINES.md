# App Store Description Guidelines

Guidance for writing the Rummy Scorekeeper App Store description and marketing copy. Ensures compliance with Apple's guidelines around gambling and real-money content.

---

## Avoid These Terms

- betting
- gambling
- wager
- real money
- stakes
- cash games
- gambling app

---

## Use Instead

- scorekeeping
- friendly games
- tracking
- illustrative amounts
- for fun only
- friendly card games
- score tracking

---

## Suggested App Store Description Phrasing

> Track scores and settlements for friendly Indian Rummy games. Dollar amounts are for fun only; no real money is involved.

Or variations:

> Rummy Scorekeeper helps you track scores and who owes whom during friendly Indian Rummy games. Point values and dollar amounts shown are illustrativeâ€”for fun only, no real money involved.

---

## Summary

The app is a **scorekeeping and tracking tool** for friendly card games. Emphasize that dollar amounts are illustrative and no real money changes hands.
